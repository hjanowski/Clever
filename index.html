Our Team

Meet the innovative minds behind Clever AI - a team of experts dedicated to revolutionizing how we understand and interact with information through advanced visualization technology.

Rami Segal

Co-Founder & CEO

With over 15 years of experience in AI and machine learning, Rami leads Clever's vision and strategy. Prior to founding Clever, he served as Head of AI Research at TechVision, where he pioneered breakthrough algorithms in natural language understanding. Rami holds a PhD in Computer Science from MIT and has published extensively on visual representation of language models.

Hadas Yaniv

Co-Founder & CTO

Hadas is the technical mastermind behind Clever's innovative visualization engine. Her background in computational linguistics and information design allows her to bridge the gap between complex data structures and intuitive visual representations. Before Clever, Hadas developed proprietary visualization systems at Google and led the UX research team at DataViz Innovations. She holds an MSc in Computational Linguistics from Stanford University.

Alon Haze

Co-Founder & CPO

As Chief Product Officer, Alon shapes how users experience Clever's technology across platforms. His background in cognitive psychology and human-computer interaction gives him unique insight into how people process visual information. Alon previously led product teams at Adobe, where he helped develop their Creative Cloud visualization tools. He is passionate about making complex information accessible through thoughtful design and holds a dual degree in Psychology and Design from Rhode Island School of Design.

Noa Ziglin

Co-Founder & COO

Noa brings strategic business acumen to Clever's operations and growth initiatives. With a background in enterprise SaaS and organizational learning, she understands how visualization technology can transform corporate knowledge management. Before Clever, Noa was VP of Business Development at EdTech pioneer LearningLens and a management consultant at McKinsey, specializing in technology adoption. She holds an MBA from Harvard Business School and remains active in the Women in AI community.

Our Mission

At Clever, we believe that information becomes knowledge when it's visualized. Our mission is to help people and organizations understand complex concepts faster by transforming raw information into intuitive visual representations. We're committed to building technology that makes learning, decision-making, and information sharing more efficient and engaging across all industries.

Clever Slides

Clever Slides is a solution designed for creating infographics optimized for slide decks. It can convert any source including articles, videos, and podcasts, and produce slide decks with infographics, architecture diagrams, flow diagrams, and more already embedded into each of the main slide topics.
